Who We Are

Hatemalo Samuha is a community-based savings and credit group established by the residents of Jwalamukhi-01, Ratamata, Dhading. We believe in the power of collective savings and mutual support to uplift every member of our community.

Our group operates on principles of transparency, democratic decision-making, and financial discipline. Every member contributes monthly, and the accumulated fund is used to provide low-interest loans to members in need.

Message from the Board

"

Hatemalo Samuha was founded on the simple belief that when a community comes together with a shared purpose, extraordinary things become possible. Over the years, we have seen our members transform their lives — starting small businesses, educating their children, and building homes — all made possible through our collective savings and mutual trust.

Our commitment is to maintain full transparency, democratic governance, and financial discipline so that every member has equal access to opportunity. We invite all residents of Jwalamukhi to join hands with us and build a stronger, more prosperous community together.
